    About the Founder

    Nampayio Koriata is a victim of FGM, teenage pregnancy, obstetric fistula and early marriage. This gave her the urge to advocate for the rights of girls and women from the pastoralist communities, mainly, the Maasai. Concerned by the illiteracy level, lack of awareness and ignorance on sexual and reproductive health rights among Maasai community, Nampayio felt the need to establish the Trust so as to reach out and assist women who silently suffer from obstetric fistula and suffer from low self-esteem.
    So far, the Trust has been able to identify and assist in facilitating over 300 women for corrective surgeries mostly performed at Kenyatta National Hospital. With adequate funding, the Trust aims to reach out to more women with such cases.
